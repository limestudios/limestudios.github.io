How I tackled the Simply Susan Site.

About the Client

Born and raised in New Orleans, Susan de la Houssaye grew up in a city of collectors - antiques, art, Mardi Gras beads, and whatever people could get their hands on. All she ever wanted to do was organize them into piles, colors, stacks or storage bins. If ever a drawer or closet was cluttered, she wanted to rearrange it to make it harmonious.

Project Scope

I was hired by Susan to completely redo her businesses' website simplysusansite.com
